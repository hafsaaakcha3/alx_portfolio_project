Types of Food Categories

Modern Cooking

Modern cooking refers to contemporary techniques and styles that emphasize new flavors, technology, and innovation. It often includes molecular gastronomy, sous-vide cooking, and fusion cuisine, which combines elements from various culinary traditions.

Traditional Cooking

Traditional cooking encompasses methods and recipes passed down through generations. It often highlights regional ingredients and time-honored techniques, such as slow cooking, fermentation, and the use of traditional spices and herbs.

Sweets

Sweets are delightful treats that typically contain sugar or other sweeteners. This category includes desserts like chocolates, candies, pastries, and ice creams. They are often enjoyed as a part of celebrations or as a special indulgence.

Saltines

Saltines are savory snacks that are usually crispy and lightly salted. This category includes crackers, pretzels, and savory biscuits that are enjoyed as appetizers, with dips, or as a complement to soups and salads.

Cakes

Cakes are baked desserts that come in a variety of flavors and styles, including sponge cakes, cheesecakes, and layer cakes. They are often decorated with frosting, fruits, and nuts, and are a centerpiece for celebrations such as birthdays and weddings.
